## 1. Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) defines the controlled process for manufacturing Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) at Sai Life Sciences' Hyderabad facility in accordance with **ICH Q7** Good Manufacturing Practice for Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, **21 CFR 211** Current Good Manufacturing Practice for Finished Pharmaceuticals, and **EU GMP Part II** (chapters 1-19).

## 2. Scope

Applies to manufacturing of non-sterile APIs in production block 2 covering reaction, isolation, drying, and bulk packaging unit operations. Excludes sterile API manufacturing (governed by SOP-MFG-002).

## 4. Procedure (high-level)

1. **Batch initiation:** Operator retrieves controlled Master Batch Record (MBR) version 4.2; QA verifies revision currency against the controlled-doc index.
2. **Material verification:** Each charged raw material's CoA is verified against approved supplier list. Material identity confirmed by IR or HPLC per material spec.
3. **Process execution:** Each unit operation completed per validated process parameters logged in BMR; deviations captured in deviation system.
4. **In-process controls:** IPC samples drawn at each phase transition per sampling plan; results entered in LIMS within 4 hours.
5. **Phase gating:** No phase progression until prior phase IPC results meet acceptance criteria; deviations require QA-approved investigation.
6. **Drying & milling:** Per equipment-specific SOPs; particle-size analysis performed pre and post milling.
7. **Bulk packaging:** Tared drums; net weight verified against batch theoretical yield; label per labelling SOP-LBL-007.
8. **Batch closure:** BMR review by Production then QA; closure within 30 calendar days of last operation.
9. **Release decision:** QA Batch Release reviews complete BMR, CoA, deviations, change controls; documented release decision in QMS.
